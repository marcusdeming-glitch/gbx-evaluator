ℹ️ About This Tool

This tool evaluates tender proposals against the GBX procurement system tender (ITT FINHQ0ETT26000001). Upload a bidder's submission in any format (PDF, Word, Excel, ZIP bundles), and Claude AI will assess it against the 4-stage evaluation framework, identify gaps, and flag items requiring human review or bidder clarification.

  • Stage 1 — Critical pass/fail criteria (debarment, GRA, briefing, Ivalua SaaS)
  • Stage 2 — Proposal quality scoring (100 points across 7 criteria)
  • Submission completeness check (18 required sections)
  • Downloadable PDF and Excel evaluation reports
  • Chat Q&A interface for querying specific details
